iPhone Photos

You really don't need a fancy camera or expensive equipment to be a photographer. Sure, they help when you're trying to get sharper quality and play around in different lighting or with fast-moving subjects. However, as an amateur photographer, my phone has served me well and is much more convenient to carry around.

Benefits of using an iPhone

  • Compact and easy to carry around
  • User friendly with automatic lighting settings
  • Efficient sharing options
